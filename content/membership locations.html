Purpose

BPDM is an organization that encourages minority participation
and aims to foster mentorship, guidance, and connections to
underrepresented groups in Data Mining. Participation is encouraged
through annual meetings and scholarly outreach.

Participants from all over the world have gotten a chance to connect
with needed intellectual resource that has provided career insight.
Students across continental North and South America and as
far as Europe have joined as members. The Google map below shows
current membership distribution.
